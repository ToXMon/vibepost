## Problem solved (2–3 sentences)
Developers using AI tools are flooded with scattered, low-signal content and no focused way to follow high-quality implementation learnings. VibePost solves this with a polished publishing SaaS that combines wallet-native author authentication, subscriber capture, premium monetization flows, and IPFS-backed post persistence in one product surface. Instead of random social posts, creators can run a repeatable, revenue-ready content business around what they build.
